Submission Instructions

You are requested to submit your research paper using the EasyChair Submission Portal: https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=6thiscls. The submissions will be reviewed by the program committee members. If you have not used EasyChair before, you may need to sign up and create a free account in EasyChair before submitting the draft.

To prepare your submission, use the latex source files:

  • Combined Zip File: inst.zip
  • Overleaf Template: overleaf
  • Sample Tex Source File: iscls19.tex
  • Sample PDF file: iscls.pdf
  • Style file: scl.sty
  • Bibiliography Style: acl.bst
  • Sample Bibiliography file: iscls19.bib

Instructions for compilation:
Let the fileame of your submision be ABC.tex

xelatex ABC
bibtex ABC
bibtex ABC (Remember to run bibtex twice)
xelatex ABC
